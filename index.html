About WarmKiwi

Your one-stop
Healthy Homes

At WarmKiwi, we offer a stress-free Healthy Homes Standards solution — ensuring your rental property is fully compliant, warm, and healthy, so landlords and tenants can relax.

Our inspectors are trained by HPA and the HomeFit programme by NZGBC, with extensive knowledge and experience in home performance.

We provide a wide range of property maintenance services: insulation, ground moisture barriers, heat pumps, heaters, guttering, rangehoods, exhaust fans, and more.
