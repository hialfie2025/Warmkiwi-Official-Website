Draught Stopping Standard

Draughts make homes cold, uncomfortable, and harder to heat. The HHS draught stopping standard requires landlords to block unreasonable gaps and holes in walls, ceilings, floors, and around doors and windows that cause draughts.

What needs to be sealed?

  • Gaps and holes in walls, ceilings and floors
  • Around window and door frames
  • Around pipes, wires and vents
  • Unused fireplaces and chimneys (where applicable)

What we provide

Our team will assess your property and seal all identified gaps and draughts using appropriate materials, ensuring your property meets the HHS draught stopping standard and is warmer and more energy-efficient for tenants.
