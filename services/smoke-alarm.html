Smoke Alarm Requirements

All rental properties in New Zealand are required to have working smoke alarms. From 1 July 2021, rental properties must have long-life photoelectric smoke alarms installed in specific locations.

What we provide

  • Supply and installation of compliant smoke alarms
  • Placement in all required locations
  • Replacement of expired or non-compliant alarms
  • Confirmation of compliance for your records

Contact us today to ensure your property's smoke alarms meet current requirements. This is one of the quickest and most affordable steps to HHS compliance.
