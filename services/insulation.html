Insulation Standard

The Healthy Homes Standards require rental properties to have ceiling and underfloor insulation that meets minimum R-value requirements. Proper insulation keeps your home warm in winter, cool in summer, and reduces energy bills.

We supply and install insulation products that meet or exceed HHS requirements, ensuring your property is fully compliant.

Ground Moisture Barrier

Properties with a suspended timber floor (subfloor space) must have a ground moisture barrier installed to prevent moisture rising from the ground into the home. This helps protect the structure of your property and improves the health and comfort of tenants.

  • Ceiling insulation installation
  • Underfloor insulation installation
  • Ground moisture barrier (polythene sheet)
  • Compliance with HHS insulation requirements
