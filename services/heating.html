Meeting the Heating Standard

In order to meet Healthy Homes Standards, one or more qualified heaters with a certain minimum heating capacity is needed in your main living area.

We work with big brands such as Mitsubishi, Panasonic, GREE, Daikin and more to provide heat pumps and installation, helping you meet the requirements.

Fixed Electric Heaters

In some cases, a fixed electric heater can be used as an alternative or for topping up existing heating capacity. Contact us for a free consultation — we'll explain:

  • How the minimum heating capacity is calculated
  • What qualifies as a "qualified heater"
  • What if you have two living areas or stairs connecting two levels
  • Whether underfloor heating or a fireplace counts
  • Your options if existing heating capacity is insufficient
